Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

To add a new property listing, navigate to the Properties tab and click the "Add New Property" button. Fill out all required information in the form, upload photos, and set the listing status. Once complete, click "Save" to publish your listing.

All commission payments can be tracked in the Commissions tab. You'll see a history of earned commissions, pending payments, and detailed information about each transaction. You can also generate reports for specific time periods.

Our platform offers several marketing tools including email campaigns, social media integration, printable flyers, and market reports. These can be accessed through the Marketing section in the main menu.

You can schedule viewings through the Appointments tab. Click "New Appointment", select the property and client, then choose a date and time. The system will send automatic reminders to both you and the client.

Comprehensive market data is available in the Market Reports section. You can view trends by region, property type, and time period. Custom reports can also be generated for specific client needs.
